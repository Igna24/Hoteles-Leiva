Olia Hotel

Free Parking Per Friendly Swimming pool Restaurant Balcony
Sea views Air conditioner Private bathroom Terrace Views

Olia Hotel is located 200 meters from the port of Tourlos in Mykonos. It has a bar
and outdoor pool. Its rooms include air conditioning and a terrace or balcony
overlooking the garden or the Aegean Sea.
Rooms at the Olia have simple décor and are equipped with satellite TV, a safe, a
fridge and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.
Guests can have refreshing drinks at the bar or on the sun terrace surrounding the
pool. The lively center of Mykonos is less than 1.5 km away and offers a variety of
restaurants, bars and cafes.
Staff at the 24-hour reception can help you rent a car and provide information on
beaches such as Agios Stefanos, just 1 km away, or Paradise, 5 km away. There is
also an internet corner with Wi-Fi connection.
Olia Hotel offers a free shuttle service to and from Mykonos Airport, located 2.5 km
away. There is also free parking.
